### Creative Direction

The project uses a dark editorial control-surface aesthetic for media management. The stated creative north star is "The Digital Curator": cinematic, spacious, and content-first rather than generic SaaS chrome.

### System Notes

- Prefer tonal separation over hard borders.
- Use restrained glass/blurring only on floating layers.
- Default buttons use a subtle `primary_dim -> primary` gradient.
- Inputs and controls should live on elevated dark surfaces.
- High-contrast white is softened to `#f9f9fd` for reduced glare.
